FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Poor picture quality
You may encounter an LED TV that will go blank, and nothing seems to turn the display back on. Even a quick reboot won’t fix things. If this happens to you, it is likely that the power supply part of your LED TV is damaged.
TV On and Off Problem
TV turning on and off problems are often caused by a loose connection between the back screen and the front one. This can be caused by a loose connection in the back.
Damage Screen
A damaged screen on television means that a part of your TV is damaged. Have you ever had a smart TV or similar device whose screen won’t turn on or work properly? This problem is usually caused by a damaged screen.
TV Remote Not Working
TV remote not working is a common problem in today’s digital world. You may have tried replacing the batteries or replacing the remote itself, but nothing seems to work again. Even if your TV is new and was never used with another remote, it may still need to be replaced.
If the Internet is not working on the TV?
If the internet is not working on TV, then it means that there is a problem with your personal or business network. The main reason for this is that there may be some physical problems at the workplace. Or, more commonly, it is caused by a configuration error that has caused the driver to stop working properly.
Sound Problem
The sound problem is common in some branded TV. This can be caused by a faulty HDMI cable, a cable that is not of sufficient length, or the presence of interference on your home network.
No Picture
No picture on the TV screen would mean that the TV is unable to receive the image. This can be caused by a number of different problems, but one major reason can be due to poor signal reception.
Vertical and Horizontal Lines On the Screen
LED TV requires a signal to display the view on the screen which it gets from the set-up box and eventually via satellite. If the signal strength from the satellite or set-up box is low, the screen starts showing horizontal lines as it does by default. Like horizontal lines, there are some other cases when the screen shows vertical lines. Because of the LED panel present in it, this type of problem occurs. The problem persists because of some fault in that panel.
TV Repair Cost
TV repair costs depend on a number of factors, including the age and condition of the product. It also depends on the model type and brand name.
Is CRT TV Repairable
Yes, a CRT TV is repairable. If your TV is still under warranty and has a problem not covered by the warranty, it may be possible to have it repaired by an authorized service center.
